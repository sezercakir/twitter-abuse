Hashtag Abuser Project V1.0

This project aims to provide better twitter stream for user.

Twitter is by far the most used social network today. Countless bot accounts have been created on a network where a post receives millions of interactions within seconds. And when real users, who are no different from thousands of bot accounts, are added to this, it negatively affects our use of twitter.

After signing in with the Twitter account registered in your browser, you authorize the project on behalf of your own account. The current trending topics are pulled from the Twitter api and the tweets inside each trending topic are also pulled. The resulting topic and tweets belonging to the topic are passed through the abuser detection algorithms and the Twitter user who uses the topic for other than its purpose is blocked on your behalf.
